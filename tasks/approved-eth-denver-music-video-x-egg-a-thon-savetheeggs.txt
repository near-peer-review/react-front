   Project Overview: Presentation with a short High-Level 6 bullet points
   summary:

   The Road to ETH Denver and Egg-a-thon is a collaboration inspired by
   the $BLACKDRAGON community meme token. It will be used to leverage
   several projects while creating an educational platform for musicians
   in a renewed approach to Near x Music.
     * A music video will be released via ShardDog at ETH Denver
     * Each video QR copy contains dragon eggs from NEAR DragonVerse
       collection.
     * Eggs received will be randomized by 1, 6, 12, or 13
     * A Golden Egg grand prize. TBD
     * QR Codes will randomize alternate album covers.

   Music Education

   The music industry is still much of an enigma, even to the artists
   themselves. We are using this as a chance to begin creating a stronger
   foundation for educating artists, especially musicians about the
   opportunities brought to them by web3.

   Core Themes:
     * New Distribution Models Offered in Web3
     * Artist Royalties
     * Masters
     * Building and Owning Your Economy

   To further expand on our experiential context to these themes, we will
   be auctioning off the MASTERS of the song and music video recorded by
   David Morrison on the new Near x Music page in collaboration with
   Mintbase.

   Additionally, we will be doing podcast interviews and hosting Twitter
   spaces with key influencer accounts to publicize the auction further
   and discuss the advantages artists have moving on-chain.
